⚔ The Age of Fire

— from the First Flame to the Curse —

In the Age of Ancients, the world was unformed, shrouded by fog. A land of gray crags, Archtrees, and Everlasting Dragons. But then there was Fire, and with it came Disparity: Heat and cold, life and death, and of course... Light and Dark. From the Dark, They came, and found the Souls of Lords within the flame... [citation:3]

The First Flame

The primordial spark that introduced disparity to a gray, static reality. Out of its heat, the Lord Souls materialized, laying down the foundational pillars of light, death, and chaotic flame.

The Lords

Gwyn, Nito, the Witch of Izalith, and the Furtive Pygmy seized the immense powers within the Fire. Betrayed by Seath, they crushed the immortal dragons to rule the world [citation:4].

The Fading

As the First Flame wanes, light recedes and the dark takes dominion. Gwyn committed the ultimate sin by sacrifice, attempting to link the fire and cursing humanity to cycles of hollowing.

The Chosen Undead

An outcast brandished with the curse, prophesied to escape the Asylum. Propelled through a dying kingdom, you are left to choose whether to feed the flame or initiate the age of dark.

⚔ Covenants

— the factions that shape the fate of Lordran —

Covenants are factions that players can align with, offering unique benefits, items, and multiplayer interactions. Each covenant is deeply rooted in the game's lore and reflects different philosophies and allegiances [citation:4].

🌀 Key Items

— the artifacts that define the Undead journey —

The Darksign

A branding representing the curse of the Undead [citation:2]. It ties humanity directly to bonfires, sending the cursed back to safety at the costly sacrifice of accumulated souls and humanity [citation:2].

Humanity

Black sprites found deep inside human bosoms [citation:1]. They define core item discovery rates and resistances [citation:1]. Consuming humanity reverts hollowing but risks total loss upon unexpected death [citation:1].

The Lordvessel

An relic bestowed by Gwynevere, allowing warping between distant bonfires [citation:10]. The vessel is required to gather the Lord Souls to break the sealed gates of the Kiln of the First Flame [citation:3].

Souls

The universal currency and life energy extracted from fallen victims [citation:6]. Required for level progression and weapon ascension, souls represent the burden and struggle of the Undead journey [citation:6].
